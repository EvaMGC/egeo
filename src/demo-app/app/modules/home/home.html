Egeo Design Guide

Creating the statement for a scalable and consistent design guide.

Egeo is an open source design system that combines both the world of design and development in one single interface. This product works together with UX/UI guidelines and the Angular 2 Library.

Egeo’s design looks for an innovative, smart and balanced line, without forgetting the professional and reliable side.

We also provide Egeo Themes to help you creating and personalazing your theme wuth your own colors. Furthermore, thanks to “Egeo Starter” you can start using a brand new Angular 2 app with a set of well-designed and implemented data-bound components built on top of Angular.
