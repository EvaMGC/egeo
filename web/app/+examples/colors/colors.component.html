Colors

The colors of Stratio's interface products have been selected to convey the brand personality and values. We have created a palette which includes Stratio core colours through blue accents juxtaposed on neutral environments, mainly whites & greys. This combination reflects clarity, simplicity, intelligence, communication, trust, efficiency & logic.
